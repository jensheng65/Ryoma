dMRI

What is diffusion MRI?

Diffusion MRI (dMRI) measures the microscopic movement of water molecules along the brain's white-matter fibers. Because water diffuses differently through healthy versus degrading neural tissue, dMRI reveals the structural integrity of your brain's wiring — without contrast agents, radiation, or injections.

  • Non-invasive & radiation-free
  • Maps white-matter micro-architecture
  • Detects change before it becomes visible on standard MRI
Diffusion tensor fiber tracking
Age™

How the Brain Age™ algorithm works

The AcroViz algorithm — FDA-cleared and TFDA-certified — analyzes your diffusion data against a validated reference population to compute your Neurophysiological Brain Age. The gap between your chronological age and your Brain Age is the single most powerful early indicator of cognitive trajectory.

  • Trained on clinically validated reference cohorts
  • Outputs an objective, quantitative Brain Age score
  • Surfaces risk up to a decade before clinical symptoms
